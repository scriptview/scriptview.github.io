Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

ScriptView is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Your scripts and reading preferences stay on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information.

ScriptView operates entirely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics services
  • Display advertisements
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Transmit any data to external servers

Data Stored on Your Device

ScriptView stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Script files: The .fountain files you import are stored in the app's documents folder on your device.
  • Reading progress: Your reading position in each script is saved locally so you can continue where you left off.
  • App preferences: Basic settings are stored using iOS's standard preferences system.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

ScriptView does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions completely independently without any external dependencies.

Flutter Framework

ScriptView is built using Flutter, an open-source UI framework developed by Google. We want to be transparent about this:

  • No Google services: While Flutter is developed by Google, ScriptView does not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Google Play Services, or any other Google services.
  • No data transmission: Flutter as a framework does not collect or transmit user data. It is simply a tool used to build the app's user interface.
  • Open source: Flutter is open-source software, and its code can be reviewed at github.com/flutter/flutter.

For more information about Flutter's privacy practices, visit flutter.dev.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. Your scripts and reading habits are yours alone.

Children's Privacy

ScriptView does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes, so we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
