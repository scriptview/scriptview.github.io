About ScriptView

ScriptView is a simple, elegant app for reading screenplays written in the Fountain format. Whether you're a screenwriter reviewing your work, an actor studying a script, or a film enthusiast exploring classic screenplays, ScriptView provides a distraction-free reading experience.

The app features a warm, paper-like aesthetic designed to reduce eye strain during extended reading sessions, with proper screenplay formatting that respects the craft of screenwriting.

Features

Fountain Format Support

Full support for the Fountain screenplay format, including scene headings, dialogue, action, transitions, and more.

Reading Progress

Automatically saves your reading position so you can pick up right where you left off.

Import Scripts

Easily import .fountain files from the Files app or other apps using the standard iOS share sheet.

Offline Reading

All your scripts are stored locally on your device. No internet connection required, no account needed.

Eye-Friendly Design

Warm, cream-colored background inspired by traditional screenplay paper to reduce eye strain.

Completely Free

No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions. Just a clean reading experience.

What is Fountain?

Fountain is a simple, plain-text markup language for writing screenplays. It allows writers to focus on their story without worrying about complex formatting software. ScriptView faithfully renders Fountain files with proper screenplay formatting.

Learn more about the Fountain format at fountain.io.

Built with Flutter

ScriptView is built using Flutter, Google's open-source UI framework. While Flutter is developed by Google, ScriptView does not use any Google services, analytics, or data collection. The framework is used purely for building the user interface.

Flutter itself does not collect any user data. See Flutter's policies for more information about the framework.
