Before You Write

Here are some common topics that might help:

  • Importing scripts: Use AirDrop, iCloud Drive, or the Files app to import .fountain files. You can also use "Open in ScriptView" from any app that shares files.
  • Reading progress: Your position is saved automatically when you leave a script. Just tap on the script again to continue where you left off.
  • File format: ScriptView only supports the Fountain format (.fountain files). Learn more at fountain.io.
  • Deleting scripts: Use the Files app to manage scripts stored in ScriptView's documents folder.

When Contacting Support

To help us assist you quickly, please include:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15)
  • iOS version (found in Settings > General > About)
  • A description of the issue or question
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, if applicable
